Meeting Purpose

Discuss design philosophy, processes, and content creation for designers

Key Takeaways

  • Developing a comprehensive design philosophy is crucial for creating a unique agency positioning and maintaining consistent quality
  • Writing down abstract thoughts helps clarify ideas and develop a coherent philosophy
  • There's a gap in content for intermediate designers transitioning from technical skills to deeper design understanding
  • Western philosophy can provide valuable mental models for developing design thinking

Topics

Design Philosophy Development

  • Ru is creating a comprehensive "superstructure" for his design philosophy
  • Includes ontological stance, core values, principles, and methodologies
  • Aims to tie philosophy to practice through a structured framework
  • Helps maintain consistency, quality, and a unique market position
  • Process involves deep introspection and answering fundamental questions about design

Writing and Organizing Thoughts

  • Uses multiple tools: Google Docs for writing, FigJam for visual organization
  • Process is non-linear, involving back-and-forth between tools
  • ChatGPT/Claude used for quick research and idea exploration
  • Writing down abstract thoughts helps clarify and develop ideas

Client Communication

  • High-level philosophy isn't typically shared with clients
  • Focus on outcomes, deliverables, and tangible value for client discussions
  • Project scaffold concept: agree on measurable criteria at project start

Content Creation for Designers

  • Gap identified in content for intermediate designers
  • Potential topics: design processes, fundamental nature of design, reading fiction for narrative skills
  • Challenge: balancing abstract concepts with practical skills that viewers seek

Design Education and Growth

  • Importance of reading widely, especially fiction, for narrative skills
  • Need for designers to understand the broader context and potential of design
  • Value of iterating and exploring multiple design options (e.g. 55 versions of a letterhead)

Next Steps

  • Ru to continue developing and documenting his design philosophy superstructure
  • Value to refine target audience and content strategy for design-focused videos
  • Both to explore Western philosophy for additional mental models and design insights
  • Reconnect in two weeks to share progress and new ideas
